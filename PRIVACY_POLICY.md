We collect this information when you voluntarily agree to do so when you use our Service.  
You may disable telemetry within the Misono Configurator, which will also wipe instantly all data tied to you **with the following exclusions**:

- The last date and time you've fetched Misono (***this is required so we don't accidentally wipe your account for inactivity***, we however only store **the date**, not the precise hour down to the second)

- **Right to Data Portability** — You have the right to request that we transfer the data we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at contact@sirio-network.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.
